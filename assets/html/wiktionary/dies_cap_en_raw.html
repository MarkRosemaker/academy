Etymology[edit]

From Latin dies (“"Day"”)

Proper noun[edit]

Dies

  1. (Roman mythology) One of the Protogenoi, the Protogenos personification of day. She is the daughter of fellow Protogenoi, Nox/Nyx (Night) and Erebus (Darkness), and sister-wife of Aether. She is the Roman counterpart of Hemera.
