German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Middle High German bereite, from Old High German bireiti, reiti; from the Proto-Germanic base *raidaz (“ready”). Cognate with English ready.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [bəˈʁaɪ̯t]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

bereit (not comparable)

  1. ready
