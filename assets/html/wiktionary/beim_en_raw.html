German[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /baɪ̯m/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -aɪ̯m

Contraction[edit]

beim

  1. Contraction of bei dem: at the, by the, etc. (followed by a masculine or neuter singular noun in the dative case)
    Das Postamt befindet sich beim Hauptbahnhof.
    The post office is near the main train station.
