  • IPA(key): /ˈvaɪ̯zə/

Etymology 1[edit]

From Old High German wīsa, from Proto-Germanic *wīsaz (“wise”)

Noun[edit]

Weise f (genitive Weise, plural Weisen)

  1. way, manner
  2. (music) tune
  3. (grammar) mode/mood (of verbs)
  4. (archaic, poetic) a song that tells a story
