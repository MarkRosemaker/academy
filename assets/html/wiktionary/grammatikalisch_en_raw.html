German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Latin grammaticalis. Analysable as Grammatik +‎ -al +‎ -isch.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˌɡʁamatiˈkaːlɪʃ/, /ɡʁaˌmatiˈkaːlɪʃ/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

grammatikalisch (comparative grammatikalischer, superlative am grammatikalischsten)

  1. grammatical
