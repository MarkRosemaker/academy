German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Wunder (“wonder”) +‎ schön (“beautiful”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): [ˈvʊndɐˈʃøːn]
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: wun‧der‧schön
  • Rhymes: -øːn

Adjective[edit]

wunderschön (comparative wunderschöner, superlative am wunderschönsten)

  1. gorgeous, very beautiful
    • 1822-1823, Heinrich Heine, Lyrisches Intermezzo
      Im wundershönen Monat Mai
      In the beautiful month of May
