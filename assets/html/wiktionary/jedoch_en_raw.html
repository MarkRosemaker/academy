German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

je +‎ doch

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /jeːˈdɔx/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb[edit]

jedoch

  1. however, yet, nevertheless, but.
    Ich liebe Schokolade, jedoch ich bevorzuge Äpfel.
    I love chocolate, but I prefer apples.

Usage notes[edit]

  • Often used as a coordinating conjunction, like und.
