lieber
better, rather

English Entry

Etymology

Comparative of lieb. Compare English liever.

Adjective

lieber

  1. comparative degree of lieb
  2. nominative singular of lieb
    (in a letter) Lieber Hans ― Dear Johnny

Adverb

lieber

  1. comparative degree of gern; rather, better, by preference
    Ich nehme lieber das Fahrrad als das Auto.
    I would rather take the bicycle than the car.
