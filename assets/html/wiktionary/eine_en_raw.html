Estonian[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Cognate to Finnish eine. Possibly a Scandinavian loan, from Proto-Norse *afnija. Compare Swedish ämne (“substance”). See also aine.

Noun[edit]

eine (genitive eine, partitive einet)

  1. (light) meal, snack
