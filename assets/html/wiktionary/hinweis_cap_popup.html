der Hinweis
[1] advice, suggestion, tip, steer
[1,2] clue, hint, notice, cue, indication
[2] evidence, lead, reference, allusion, pointer, index, guideline, link, indicator, piece of advice, piece of information, consideration

Noun

Hinweis m (genitive Hinweises, plural Hinweise)
