German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Middle High German nützelich, equivalent to Nutzen +‎ -lich.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈnʏtslɪç/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adjective[edit]

nützlich (comparative nützlicher, superlative am nützlichsten)

  1. useful; helpful
