damit
so that, in order to

English Entry

Etymology

From da +‎ mit. Compare English theremid, therewith.

Adverb

damit

  1. pronominal adverb of mit

Conjunction

damit

  1. (subordinating) so that, in order that
    Beeil dich, damit wir bald losfahren können.
    Hurry up so that we can take off soon.
Synonyms
  • dass (chiefly in colloquial usage)
