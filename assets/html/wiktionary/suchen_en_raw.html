German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Old High German suohhen, from Proto-Germanic *sōkijaną, from Proto-Indo-European *seh₂g- (“seek out”). Akin to Low German söken, sooken, Dutch zoeken, English seek, Danish søge, Gothic 𐍃𐍉𐌺𐌾𐌰𐌽 (sōkjan).

Verb[edit]

suchen (third-person singular simple present sucht, past tense suchte, past participle gesucht, auxiliary haben)

  1. to seek
  2. to search, to look for
    Ich suche nach meinen Schlüsseln. ― I’m looking for my keys.
