German[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • chic (standard for the uninflected form only)

Etymology[edit]

From French chic, itself from German Schick (“correct manners, correct clothing”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ʃɪk/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɪk

Adjective[edit]

schick (comparative schicker, superlative am schicksten)

  1. elegant, dressy
