German[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • gross (Switzerland, Luxembourg, Liechtenstein)

Etymology[edit]

From Old High German grōz, akin to Old Saxon grōt, compare Dutch groot. More at English great.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ɡʁoːs/
  • audio (Germany)
    		(file)
  • audio (Austria)
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -oːs

Adjective[edit]

groß (comparative größer, superlative am größten)

  1. big, large
  2. great, grand
  3. (of a person) tall
