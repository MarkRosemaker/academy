German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

an +‎ wenden

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈʔanvɛndn̩/, /ˈʔanvɛndən/
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: an‧wen‧den

Verb[edit]

anwenden (irregular, third-person singular simple present wendet an, past tense wandte an or wendete an, past participle angewandt or angewendet, auxiliary haben)

  1. to use, apply, utilize or deploy (to put to use for a purpose)
