English Entry

Adjective

geschlossen (comparative geschlossener, superlative am geschlossensten)

  1. shut; closed; locked
  2. (of a party, society) private, non-public
  3. (of a community, group, team) cohesive; solidary
Usage notes
  • With doors, German does not necessarily distinguish between “shut” and “locked”, though the latter is often specified by the word abgeschlossen.
Synonyms
  • (closed): zu
  • (private): privat
  • (cohesive): solidarisch; (eng) zusammenhaltend; zusammenstehend
Derived terms
  • Geschlossenheit

Verb

geschlossen

  1. Past participle of schließen.
