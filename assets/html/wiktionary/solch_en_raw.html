German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Old High German sulih, solih, from Proto-Germanic *swalīkaz.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /zɔʏ̯ç/ (regional variant, especially in casual speech)
  • IPA(key): /sɔlx/, /solx/ (mainly Austro-Bavarian and Swiss)
