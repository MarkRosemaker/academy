Verb[edit]

ansehen (class 5 strong, third-person singular simple present sieht an, past tense sah an, past participle angesehen, past subjunctive sähe an, auxiliary haben)

  1. to look at
  2. (figuratively) to regard
  3. (reflexive) to view
