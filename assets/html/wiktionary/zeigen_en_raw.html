German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Middle High German zeigen, from Old High German zeigōn. Probably derived from an ancestor of zeihen. Ultimately related to Proto-Germanic *taikijaną. Cognate with English teach, Gothic 𐌲𐌰𐍄𐌴𐌹𐌷𐌰𐌽 (gateihan).

Verb[edit]

zeigen (third-person singular simple present zeigt, past tense zeigte, past participle gezeigt, auxiliary haben)

  1. to point at
  2. to demonstrate, show
    Synonyms: demonstrieren, vorführen
  3. (reflexive) to appear, become apparent, come out, turn out, show up, to be manifested
