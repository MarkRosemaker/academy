German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Old High German bileggen, equivalent to be +‎ legen. Cognate with English belay.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /bəˈleːɡŋ̩/, /bəˈleːɡən/
  • Audio (Austria)
    		(file)
  • Audio
    		(file)
  • Hyphenation: be‧le‧gen

Verb[edit]

belegen (third-person singular simple present belegt, past tense belegte, past participle belegt, auxiliary haben)

  1. to document, back, substantiate
  2. to occupy
