die Hemmung

English Entry

Etymology

From hemmen +‎ -ung.

Noun

Hemmung f (genitive Hemmung, plural Hemmungen)

  1. (psychology) inhibition
  2. scruple
  3. hindrance, hindering
  4. escapement of a timepiece
