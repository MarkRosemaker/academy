German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Old High German zil, from the original *til. Cognate to Old English til

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /tsiːl/
  • audio
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -iːl

Noun[edit]

Ziel n (genitive Zieles, plural Ziele)

  1. target
  2. goal (not in football, see Tor)
  3. aim
  4. purpose
