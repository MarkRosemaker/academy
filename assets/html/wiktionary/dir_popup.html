dir
you, thou/thee

English Entry

Etymology

From Middle High German dir, from Old High German dir, from Proto-Germanic *þiz.

Pronoun

dir

  1. (personal) dative of du; you, to you.
  2. (reflexive) dative; yourself, to yourself.
