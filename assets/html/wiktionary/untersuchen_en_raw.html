German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

unter- +‎ suchen. Cognate with Dutch onderzoeken, English underseek, Hunsrik unnersuche.

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ʔʊntɐˈzuːxən/, [ʔʊntɐˈzuːxn̩]
  • Audio
    		(file)

Verb[edit]

untersuchen (third-person singular simple present untersucht, past tense untersuchte, past participle untersucht, auxiliary haben)

  1. to examine, to investigate
