Translingual[edit]

Symbol[edit]

DO

  1. The ISO 3166-1 two-letter (alpha-2) code for Dominican Republic.

English[edit]

Noun[edit]

DO (countable and uncountable, plural DOs)

  1. (grammar) a direct object
  2. (US) a Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine
  3. (sciences) dissolved oxygen
  4. (dentistry) Initialism of disto occlusal.
