German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

da (“there”) (from dar) + über (“over”)

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /daˈʁyːbɐ/
  • Audio
    		(file)

Adverb[edit]

darüber

  1. above it, above that, thereover
  2. about something; concerning a matter

Antonyms[edit]

  • darunter
