German[edit]

Etymology[edit]

er- +‎ freuen

  • Audio

  • Hyphenation: er‧freu‧en

Verb[edit]

erfreuen (third-person singular simple present erfreut, past tense erfreute, past participle erfreut, auxiliary haben)

  1. to please
  2. (reflexive) to enjoy
