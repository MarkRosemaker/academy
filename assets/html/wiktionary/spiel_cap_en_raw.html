German[edit]

Spiel

Etymology[edit]

From Middle High German spil, from Old High German spil, from Proto-Germanic *spilą (“dance”).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ʃpiːl/
  • Rhymes: -iːl
  • audio (Austria)
    		(file)
  • Audio
    		(file)

Noun[edit]

Spiel n (genitive Spieles, plural Spiele, diminutive Spielchen n) (countable as for specific games, uncountable as an activity)

  1. game, play
  2. (of mechanical parts only, never figuratively) leeway, wiggle room
  3. (Switzerland) military band, marching band
