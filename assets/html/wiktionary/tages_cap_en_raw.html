Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Etruscan 𐌕𐌀𐌓𐌙𐌉𐌄𐌔 (tarχies).

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /ˈta.ɡeːs/

Proper noun[edit]

Tagēs m sg (genitive Tagētis); third declension

  1. (mythology) An Etruscan divinity that taught the Etrurians the art of divination

Declension[edit]

Third-declension noun, singular only.
