English Entry

Etymology

From Old High German bezziro, from Proto-Germanic *batizô. Cognate with Low German beter, Dutch beter, West Frisian better, English better, Danish bedre, Sanskrit भद्र (bhadrá, “blessed, fortunate, happy, good”).

Adjective

besser

  1. comparative degree of gut; better
Related terms
  • bessern, Besserung

Verb

besser

  1. First-person singular present of bessern.
  2. Imperative singular of bessern.
