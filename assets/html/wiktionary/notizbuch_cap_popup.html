English Entry

Etymology

Notiz (“note”) +‎ Buch (“book”)

Noun

Notizbuch n (genitive Notizbuches or Notizbuchs, plural Notizbücher)

  1. notebook (book)
    Er schrieb jeden Tag in sein Notizbuch.
    He wrote in his notebook every day.

Noun (neuter)
